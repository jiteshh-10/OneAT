What is oneA ?

oneA is a one stop solution to find affordable rooms, compatible roommates, stationary, books and everything a student needs.

What is Roomie ?

Roomie connects students with compatible roommates and verified rooms, offering an affordable, secure, and community-focused alternative to traditional housing.

What is Barter ?

Barter allows students to securely trade stationery and other useful items, fostering resourcefulness, sustainability, and a sense of community on an easy-to-use platform.

How Roomie works ?

Tell us your roommate preferences (study habits, lifestyle, etc.), and our AI will find compatible matches. Chat, connect, and choose your perfect roomie – it's that easy.

How Barter work ?

List items you want to swap, browse listings from other students, find a match you like, and agree on the exchange. It's like a virtual flea market, but way cooler!

How did this idea strike us ?

The struggle to find a hostel room in Delhi University led me to create "Roomie," and while discussing swapping old notes in my college literature class, the idea for "Barter" was born! That's how OneA came into existence.
